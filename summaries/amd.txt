 ASUS accidentally published a spec listing for the AMD Ryzen AI 9 HX 170, an unknown processor, on their website. This leak suggests AMD is developing the Ryzen AI 9 HX 170 for the upcoming Zen 5-based mobile chip code-named Strix Point, which could have significant AI capabilities with an NPU integrated. The processor is part of AMD's late 2024 release plan, with full details likely to be revealed at Computex 2024.
